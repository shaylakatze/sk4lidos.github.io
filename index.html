hello.

i'm em (it/they). welcome to my everything home.

with the ongoing enshittification of the internet, i felt it important to drop the vast majority of it and focus on something that is really mine. it makes no sense that anybody has to go through several social media profiles, streaming services, and so on and so forth to actually access the work they enjoy. my goal is to provide most of what i do directly, and with as little friction as possible to both me (the author & developer) and you (the enjoyer).

you're probably here for the music and samples, though you'll find plenty more in the navigation panel on the left. happy browsing, and feel free to drop my a line via my contact information or slowchat.

for a guide on using my music in your media, please check this page.
